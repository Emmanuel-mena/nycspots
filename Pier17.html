Pier 17

Description

Set as the crown jewel of the Seaport District, The Rooftop at Pier 17 is the premiere event venue in Lower Manhattan. At five stories high and set 300ft out into the East River, The Rooftop at Pier 17 offers dramatic views unlike any other location in Manhattan. Summer concerts and ice skating rink are available to the publick. with an amazin seating view area pier 17 is most go any time of the year.
